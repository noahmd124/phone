🙄 Bad Reasons vs. My Comebacks 💪

'You'll use it at school'
School has just gone phone-free! It would be in a pouch the whole day.
'You’re too young.'
91% of 11 year olds have smart phones. It’s not just about fun — it helps me stay safe and connected when I’m out or coming home from school.
'You don’t need one.'
You don't need Andrex, or a cat, or a posh bike. It’s not about needing, it’s about learning to use it wisely early on.
'It's a distraction from schoolwork.'
I can use Do Not Disturb or focus mode when I study. Plus, I think it would help me get better at the educational things I already do, such as Duolingo & Mimo.
'Too much screentime is bad.'
I’ll follow screen time limits you set and take breaks if needed.
'You'll be exposed to bad stuff online'
You can help me set up parental controls, and I’ll only install safe apps. We can agree on rules for what I can and can’t use — and you can check my phone anytime
